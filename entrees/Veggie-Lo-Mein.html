Veggie Lo Mein

    Lo Mein

    • Sauce

    Instructions

  1. In a bowl combine together broth, brown sugar, sesame oil and soy sauce. Whisk until everything comes together. Set aside.
  2. In a preheated skillet with oil and garlic, fry carrots, onions, broccolli, and bell peppers until golden brown.
  3. Add lo mein noodles and sauce. Toss together and let lo mein cook for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Remove and serve :)
